Shattered Timeline

A Top-Down Action Roguelite Adventure

What is Shattered Timeline?

Shattered Timeline is a challenging top-down action roguelite where you fight through three increasingly difficult floors. Each run is unique, featuring procedurally generated rooms, diverse enemies, and strategic upgrade choices that test your skills and adaptability.

Lore

You are a time traveler who has been sent back in time to prevent the destruction of the timeline. But when you arrive, you realize that the timeline has already been broken. You must navigate through the timeline and prevent its destruction.

Core Features

Progressive Floors

Navigate through 3 floors, each containing 6 rooms: 4 combat areas, 1 shop, and 1 epic boss encounter.

Dual Combat System

Master both melee and ranged weapons. Switch between close-quarters dagger combat and long-range projectile attacks.

Enemy Variety

Face 5 different enemy types with unique behaviors: from aggressive Goblin Daggers to strategic Mage Goblins.

Boss Battles

Confront powerful bosses including the fearsome Dragon Boss and the tactical Supersoldier.

Upgrade Systems

Purchase temporary weapon upgrades during runs and earn permanent character improvements after defeating bosses.

Persistent Progress

Your run counter, statistics, and permanent upgrades are saved across all sessions, building your legacy over time.

Controls

Movement

W A S D Move in 8 directions
Shift Dash for enhanced mobility

Combat

1 Primary weapon (Melee)
2 Secondary weapon (Ranged)
Spacebar Attack with selected weapon

Shop Interface

W A S D Navigate shop options
Enter Purchase selected upgrade
ESC Exit shop

How to Play

1

Start Your Run

Begin in Room 1 of Floor 1 with basic equipment and 100 health points; defeat all enemies in the room to progress to the next room.

2

Clear Combat Rooms

Defeat all enemies in 4 combat rooms. Collect golden chests containing gold.

3

Visit the Shop

Spend gold on temporary upgrades: weapon damage boosts or health restoration.

4

Defeat the Boss

Face the floor boss in an epic battle. Victory unlocks permanent upgrade choices.

5

Choose Permanent Upgrades

Select from Max Health, Max Stamina, or Movement Speed improvements that persist across all future runs.

6

Progress or Reset

Advance to the next floor or restart your journey with enhanced permanent abilities.

Strategy Tips

Combat Strategy

Use melee attacks for close encounters and ranged attacks to maintain distance from dangerous enemies.

Resource Management

Save gold for crucial upgrades. Health restoration can be more valuable than damage in difficult situations.

Boss Preparation

Ensure you have sufficient weapon upgrades and full health before entering boss rooms.

Long-term Growth

Permanent upgrades compound over time. Each run makes future attempts more manageable.
